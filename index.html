BMI METHOD

BMI can tell you about excess weight. However, it can’t be an indicator of excess fat as it can’t tell the difference between excess muscle, fat, or bone. Unlike children, adult BMI is not subject to age, gender, or muscle mass. Pregnancy also affects BMI, which is why you should use pre-pregnancy weight for BMI calculation.

BMR METHOD

Your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is energy used while at rest. Two equations are used for finding BMR: Mifflin-St Jeor Equation and Harris Benedict Equation. You can switch between equations from the drop-down.
